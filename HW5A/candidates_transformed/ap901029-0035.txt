The accidental shooting death of a young stockbroker by an officer looking for a burglar is one more strike against a police force already struggling with allegations of brutality and racism .
Terry D. Barnes , 24 , was shot between the eyes in his apartment .
Police said an officer investigating a report of a prowler had entered the apartment at 3:30 a.m. and fired after Barnes got out of bed to see what was wrong .
It was the seventh shooting death involving Kansas City police officers this year .
