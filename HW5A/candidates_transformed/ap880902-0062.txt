A U.S. military jet crashed today in a remote , forested area in northern Japan , but the pilot bailed out safely and was taken by helicopter to an American military base , officials said .
The pilot of the F-16C , Maj. Wyman E. Vanedoe , was listed in good condition soon after the crash , the U.S.
Forces Japan Media Liaison Office in Tokyo said .
Vanedoe 's home town was not immediately available , said a U.S. official who spoke on condition of anonymity .
