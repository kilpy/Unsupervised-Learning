GUERRILLA tactics by Greenpeace were swamped by the White House pomp in which Mr Clinton and three past US presidents this week endorsed the North American Free Trade Agreement .
But the environmental activists , who momentarily disrupted a House hearing to unfurl a 15 foot anti-Nafta banner , saw their stunt repeated again and again on evening news shows .
With dozens of similar exploits and clever strategic planning , grassroots organisations have succeed in fanning anti-Nafta sentiment into widespread public hostility against the pact with Canada and Mexico .
A new Wall Street Journal poll , found opposition , at 36 per cent , had reached its highest level yet with only 25 per cent of those polled in favour of Nafta .
