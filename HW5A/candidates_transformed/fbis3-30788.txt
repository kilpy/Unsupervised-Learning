Language : Serbo-Croatian Article Type : BFN -LSB- Article by Verica Rudar : `` What Is Ljubljana 's Message for Belgrade ? ''
-RSB- -LSB- Text -RSB- In the view of Yugoslav diplomats , the normalization of relations between Slovenia and the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia will certainly be a strenuous and long-term project .
Although the establishment of communications between Ljubljana and Belgrade would be very important for the situation in the former Yugoslavia in general , the chances that this could happen soon are minimal .
A signal that relations between Yugoslavia and Slovenia might be reconsidered came this time from the Slovene capital .
