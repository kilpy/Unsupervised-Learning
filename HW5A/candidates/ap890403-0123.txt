_ Tornadoes travel at an average 30 mph , but can stand still or go 70 mph .
_ There were 32 tornado-related deaths reported in 1988 , down from 59 in 1987 and well below the average of 99 a year .
_ Less than 2 percent of all tornadoes are classified as violent , with wind speeds of more than 200 mph and a path averaging 26 miles .
_ When a tornado threatens , seek shelter in the basement or central parts of the house , office or school building , away from windows .
