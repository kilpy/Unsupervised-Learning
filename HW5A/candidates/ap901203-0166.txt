Over the years , he has heard countless earthquake predictions .
One woman called daily , alerting Person that an earthquake was about to occur , based on the number of birds that crossed her windshield as she drove down the street .
As Person talked , 24 seismographs etched out activity recorded by monitoring equipment in different parts of the United States , ranging from Alaska to Tennessee .
Although earthquakes can be tracked historically , forecasting them is difficult , if not impossible , Person said .
